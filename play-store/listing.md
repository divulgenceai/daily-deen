## Full Description

Daily Deen is a clean Islamic daily reading app built around a calm Sydney-time daily rhythm.

Open the app each day for a Qur'an verse, dua, dhikr, authentic hadith, quote, and short Islamic story. Each reading is presented with source labels, explanations, reflection, and a smooth focused card experience.

Features:

- Qur'an verse of the day with revelation/context notes
- Dua of the day with meaning and source-backed effect
- Dhikr of the day with a built-in tap counter
- Authentic hadith of the day with explanation
- Quote of the day from a prophet, companion, or Islamic figure
- Story of the day with a clear moral
- Saved readings for later
- Sydney-time daily lock from 12:00 AM to 12:00 AM
- Minimal black and green dark mode design

Daily Deen is designed to be simple, respectful, and easy to revisit every day.
